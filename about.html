About Capitol

An operations-led facility services company.

Capitol is built around the parts of facility services most clients only notice when they're missing: clear scheduling, real inspections, fast communication, dependable response, and standards that hold up over time.

The work behind the work

Reliable service is a system. We run it like one.

Crews show up because they're scheduled. Quality holds because it's inspected. Issues get resolved because there's a clear line of communication and someone whose job it is to answer. None of that is glamorous — but it's the difference between a vendor and a partner.

We carry a real passion for cleanliness — not just the surface kind, but the kind that shows up in detail work, restroom standards, and the corners most clients never look at. That care is what makes a building feel ready.

What we believe

The janitorial industry can be better.

Most facility complaints aren't about effort. They're about structure — unclear scopes, inconsistent crews, slow responses, and standards that drift. We're not trying to reinvent cleaning. We're trying to run it with better systems: tighter standards, clearer ideas, sharper communication, and real accountability for the work.

Clients shouldn't have to chase their vendor. Buildings shouldn't have to lower their expectations. That's the bar we hold ourselves to, and it's what every account at Capitol runs on.
