Job categories

Commercial facility roles we may consider.

Select the category that best fits your experience. If you are not sure, choose general employment interest in the application form.

Janitorial team member

Commercial cleaning support for offices, managed buildings, schools, medical spaces, warehouses, and other business facilities.

Day porter / facility support

On-site daytime support for high-traffic facilities, restrooms, common areas, supply checks, and visible upkeep during operating hours.

Floor care technician

VCT strip and wax, carpet cleaning, tile and grout care, floor maintenance, and related commercial floor service support.

Post-construction cleaning crew

Final-detail cleaning and turnover support after construction or project work, not heavy debris removal or machinery cleanup.

Painting / project support

Interior commercial painting and project-based facility refresh support when paired with cleaning, turnover, or maintenance needs.

Lead / supervisor interest

Field leadership, quality checks, team coordination, route support, and communication for commercial facility service accounts.
