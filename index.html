What happens next

A simple path from inquiry to service plan.

Commercial facilities need more than a quick price. Capitol starts with the building, the scope, and the schedule so the first conversation leads to useful next steps.

1

Share the property

Send the facility type, market, services needed, and any timing details.

2

Walkthrough or scope review

We look at access, traffic, restrooms, floor types, current pain points, and service expectations.

3

Service plan

You get a clear path for frequency, responsibilities, communication, and project timing.

4

Start and adjust

Service begins with a defined scope, then gets refined as the building’s real needs become clear.
