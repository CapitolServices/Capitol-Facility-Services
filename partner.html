Work with Capitol

For the teams hiring a facility services partner.

Capitol works alongside property managers, construction companies, general contractors, and facility teams — whether you need to add Capitol as an approved vendor, request project support, or build a long-term service relationship.

Who we partner with

Three groups, one operating standard.

The conversation looks different for each — but the underlying systems, response, and accountability are the same.

Property managers

For the buildings you manage.

Recurring janitorial, day porter, floor care, and the occasional turnover or refresh — handled with the kind of communication and QA that doesn't make work for you.

Construction & GCs

For the projects you're delivering.

Rough, final, and punch-list cleans built around the build schedule. Tight communication with site supers and project managers — no surprises before walk-through.

Facility teams

For the operators running the buildings.

An extension of your in-house team — for floor care, project work, painting, supply servicing, or filling gaps in a recurring scope.
