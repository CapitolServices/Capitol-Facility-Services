Privacy notice

Information we collect

Capitol Facility Services may collect information you choose to submit through this website, including your name, company name, email address, phone number, property address, facility type, square footage, requested services, preferred walkthrough date and time, project details, and related notes.

If you submit a partner or vendor inquiry, we may also collect business information such as service capabilities, service areas, insurance status, company details, and contact information for follow-up.

How we use information

  • To respond to service requests, walkthrough requests, partner inquiries, and general questions.
  • To evaluate whether the property, service need, location, or partnership opportunity is a good operational fit.
  • To schedule calls, site visits, walkthroughs, or follow-up communication.
  • To improve internal service planning, quality control, and customer communication.
  • To maintain business records and comply with applicable legal, tax, insurance, or operational obligations.

Information sharing

We do not sell personal information submitted through this website. We may share information only with service providers, internal team members, contractors, or advisors who need it to help respond to your request, operate the business, protect the company, or comply with law.

Sensitive information

Please do not submit medical records, patient information, protected health information, financial account numbers, government identification numbers, or other sensitive information through website forms. If a facility has special access, safety, compliance, or security requirements, share only the operational details needed for us to respond.

Retention and security

We keep submitted information only as long as reasonably needed for the purpose it was provided, business operations, legal compliance, dispute resolution, or recordkeeping. We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards, but no website or email transmission can be guaranteed completely secure.

Cookies & tracking

No nonessential cookies are currently used.

This website is currently designed without analytics scripts, advertising pixels, retargeting tags, account logins, or nonessential cookies. Because of that, the site does not currently display a cookie consent pop-up.

If analytics, advertising, embedded scheduling tools, chat widgets, or similar tracking tools are added later, this notice should be updated before those tools go live, and a cookie choice or opt-out mechanism should be added if required by applicable law or vendor behavior.

The Service Areas page may load third-party map resources to display geographic information. Those providers may receive basic technical information from your browser, such as IP address, device details, and page request information, according to their own policies.
