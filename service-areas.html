Austin metro

Commercial janitorial services in Austin, TX

Austin properties often need a flexible mix of recurring office cleaning, multi-tenant building service, day porter support, floor care, and turnover work. Capitol can discuss janitorial routes, VCT strip and wax, commercial carpet cleaning, painting, supplies, and post-construction cleaning for business facilities across the metro.

  • Downtown Austin, The Domain, East Austin, South Austin
  • Round Rock, Cedar Park, Pflugerville, Georgetown
  • Buda, Kyle, Bee Cave, Lakeway, and nearby commercial areas
San Antonio

Facility cleaning for San Antonio businesses

San Antonio commercial properties can require dependable janitorial service, restroom supply support, floor care, and project cleaning around offices, medical spaces, education facilities, showrooms, and managed properties.

  • Office and multi-tenant commercial buildings
  • Medical, education, and showroom facilities
  • Project and turnover cleaning needs
Waco

Commercial cleaning along the Central Texas corridor

Waco and nearby Central Texas facilities often sit between larger markets but still need professional service standards. Capitol can discuss janitorial cleaning, floor care, post-construction cleaning, and supply servicing for commercial properties in the area.

  • Project-based cleaning and turnover support
Houston

Commercial cleaning support for larger facility needs

Houston’s commercial market includes offices, warehouses, industrial properties, medical facilities, and large managed buildings. Service conversations may include recurring janitorial, commercial carpet cleaning, floor care, restroom supplies, painting, and post-construction cleaning.

  • Warehouse, production, and industrial facilities

  • Floor care, supplies, and project support
Dallas–Fort Worth

Janitorial and facility service conversations for DFW

Service area questions

Questions about markets and coverage.

Use the market selector on the contact form to tell us where the property is located and what type of service you need.

Yes. Austin is an important market for Capitol, and the website also highlights San Antonio, Waco, Houston, and Dallas–Fort Worth for commercial facility needs.

What Austin-area cities are included?

The Austin metro conversation can include Downtown Austin, The Domain, Round Rock, Cedar Park, Pflugerville, Georgetown, Buda, Kyle, Bee Cave, Lakeway, and nearby business areas.

Can property managers ask about more than one location?

Yes. If you manage multiple properties, use the contact form notes to share the locations, facility types, and services needed at each site.

Post-construction cleaning depends on the project location, timeline, phase of work, and the areas that need final or detail cleaning.
