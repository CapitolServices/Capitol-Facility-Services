Facility Services

Cleaning, care, and project work for commercial properties.

From recurring janitorial routes to one-off project cleanups, Capitol covers what facility teams, property managers, and builders rely on most. Every service is run on the same scheduling, inspection, and communication standards.

01 · Core service

Commercial janitorial cleaning

Recurring janitorial service is the backbone of what we do. Nightly, weekly, or custom routes built around how a property actually operates — with QA inspections that keep standards honest over time. Includes day porter coverage where buildings need a visible, in-hours presence.

Office cleaning

Recurring service for office buildings and multi-tenant commercial properties — desks, common areas, restrooms, breakrooms.

Day porter

On-site daytime coverage for high-traffic lobbies, restrooms, and shared amenity areas.

Industrial & warehouse

Production, manufacturing, and warehouse facilities — heavier scopes, off-hours scheduling, clear scope sheets.

Medical & specialized

Medical facilities, showrooms, and education facilities with elevated cleanliness or compliance standards.

02 · Floor care

Floors that hold up under traffic.

Floor care is its own discipline. We handle the full mix — soft surface, hard surface, and project-grade coatings — usually scheduled around regular janitorial so the building barely notices we were there.

VCT strip & wax

Full strip and refinish on VCT tile for offices, schools, and showrooms — done overnight or on weekends.

Carpet cleaning

Hot water extraction and spot work for carpeted office floors and high-traffic common areas.

Tile & grout

Deep clean and seal for restrooms, kitchens, and entryway tile that day-to-day cleaning can't reach.

Epoxy floor coatings

Durable epoxy systems for warehouses, production floors, and back-of-house industrial spaces.

Concrete staining

Decorative staining for showrooms, offices, and modern commercial spaces where polished concrete is the finish.

Pressure washing

Exterior pressure washing for sidewalks, dumpster pads, drive-throughs, and building exteriors.

Have VCT, carpet, tile, concrete, or exterior surfaces that need a planned refresh?

Common questions

What commercial buyers usually need to know.

Short answers for facility managers, property managers, builders, and business operators comparing service options.

Do you focus on commercial work only?

Yes. Capitol focuses on business properties, commercial facilities, managed buildings, and project-based facility needs rather than residential cleaning.

Can service happen after hours?

Schedules can be scoped around the building, access rules, operating hours, tenant activity, and project deadlines.

Do you provide supplies?

Supply servicing can be included for paper, soap, liners, and restroom or breakroom consumables when the property needs it.

Is a walkthrough required?

For most recurring or project scopes, a walkthrough or detailed scope review helps set expectations and avoid unclear pricing.

What markets do you consider?

Austin is the home market, with opportunity markets including San Antonio, Waco, Houston, and Dallas–Fort Worth.

Can multiple services be combined?

Yes. Janitorial, floor care, supplies, painting, and post-construction cleaning can be discussed together when that fits the property.

Ready to scope a job?

Tell us the property, the scope, and the timeline.
